OMICRON targets more automated, optimised road maintenance by combining robotised technologies with intelligent decision-support tools.

It explores scalable inspection and monitoring workflows to improve the speed, quality, and cost-effectiveness of road condition assessment.

It supports the transition from manual inspections to data-rich maintenance planning using digital methods.

Project website: Visit official project page

At a glance

  • Automation for inspection, monitoring, and maintenance planning
  • Digital decision support to prioritise interventions
  • Operational workflows aimed at safer, smarter road asset management
