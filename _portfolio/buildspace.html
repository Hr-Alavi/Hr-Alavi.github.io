BUILDSPACE develops space-driven services that support energy-efficient building operation and climate-resilient city planning.

It integrates digital tools and data services that inform stakeholders about performance, risk, and resilience across building and urban scales.

It enables new services that link building-level intelligence to wider city-level objectives.

At a glance

  • Digital services for energy performance and resilience decision-making
  • Cross-scale data integration from buildings to cities
  • Service-oriented approach for real-world deployment
