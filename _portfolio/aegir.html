AEGIR delivers digital and physical renovation packages to improve environmental and energy performance while optimising resource use.

It promotes incremental renovation workflows supported by digital tools—enabling better planning, execution, and follow-up through data-driven methods.

It reduces renovation friction by improving coordination, information continuity, and evidence-based decisions.

At a glance

  • Incremental renovation workflows supported by digital tools
  • Focus on energy/environmental performance and resource efficiency
  • Better coordination and data continuity across renovation stages
